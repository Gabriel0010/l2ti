UAV Path Planning

In real applications involving UAVs not being controlled by ground pilots, safety is likely to become a decisive issue. This research proposes prerequisites on the obstacles and the UAVs' control systems. The prerequisites enable a mathematical proof of UAV-UAV and UAV-obstacles avoidance in [BBDZ23] for a single UAV and in [BBD23] for a fleet.
