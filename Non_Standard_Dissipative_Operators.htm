Gabriel Dauphin (web page)

Non-Standard Dissipative Operators

Like pseudo-differential operators of diffusive type in continuous time, diffusive filters in discrete time have been introduced for the fractional difference filter and for other discretizations of fractional integrals. The impulse response of diffusive filters can be decomposed on a continuous family of geometric sequences with a decreasing weight. Using this diffusive realization, in the sense of systems theory, it helps transforming a non-local in time difference equation into a first order difference equation on an infinite-dimensional state-space, endowed with a Hilbert structure, which allows for positivity, dissipativity, asymptotic and stability analysis.
